Predicting Coronary Heart Disease by Age Group

MSc thesis · London Metropolitan University · 2023 77.17% accuracy · linear SVM, best of three models

An end-to-end predictive ML pipeline in R over clinical cardiovascular data: full ETL with data cleansing, missing-value resolution on obesity indices, categorical binarization, and feature scaling for statistical integrity.
